Indian Household Electricity Bill
Overview

An Indian Household Electricity Bill is a monthly statement issued by an electricity distribution company to domestic consumers. It provides details about electricity consumption, applicable charges, taxes, and the total amount payable for a billing period.

Key Components

Consumer Details: Name, address, consumer number, meter number

Billing Period: Start and end dates of billing

Meter Readings: Previous and current readings

Units Consumed: Electricity usage measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh)

Charges: Energy charges, fixed charges, and additional fees

Taxes & Duties: Electricity duty, GST, or state-specific taxes

Total Amount Payable: Final bill amount

Due Date: Last date for payment to avoid penalty

Purpose

The bill helps consumers track electricity usage, understand charges, and make timely payments.

Usage

This document can be used for educational projects, billing system simulations, or understanding household electricity billing in India.
